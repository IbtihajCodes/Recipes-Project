Recipe Of Biryani

Chicken Birayni

Chicken biryani is a delicious Pakistani/Indian rice dish that's typically reserved for special occasions such as weddings, parties, or holidays such as Ramadan. It has a lengthy preparation, but the work is definitely worth it. For biryani, basmati rice is the ideal variety to use.

Ingredients:

  • Vegetable Oil
  • Potatoes
  • Chopped Onions
  • Minced Garlic
  • Chicken
  • Basmati Rice
  • Plain Yogurt
  • Chopped Tomatoes
  • Cinamon Stick
  • Powdered Safron
  • Pepper Black
  • Ground Cardamom

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Heat Oil and fry the Potatoes
  3. Add remaining oil to skillet and add onions, tomatoes, salt , cumin, chilli powder, pepper and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in yogurt, mint, ground cardamom, and cinnamon. Cover and cook over low heat until tomatoes are cooked to a pulp.
  5. Add chicken and cook over low heat for 35 to 45 minutes, until the chicken is tender.
  6. Meanwhile, make the rice: Wash rice well and drain in a colander for at least 30 minutes.
  7. Heat oil in a large skillet. Add onion; cook and stir until golden.
  8. Heat stock and salt in a medium pot until hot; pour over rice and stir well.
  9. Add chicken mixture and potatoes; stir gently to combine. Bring to a boil.
  10. Reduce heat to very low, cover with a tight lid,steam for 20 minutes without lifting the lid or stirring.
  11. Spoon biryani onto a warm serving dish.
